Maisie and the Everywhere Mouse
Maisie and the Everywhere Mouse is a mix of Science Fiction and Fairy Tale with some strong themes of environmental responsibility.

Maisie, along with her Mum and Dad, eats printed food, wears printed clothes and lives in fear of a deadly virus which a century ago almost wiped out the human race.

Earth has been observed from afar for countless centuries by Post Physical Beings who love all life and occasionally send a team of Observers who, while largely unnoticed, have created our legends of Guardian Angels and Genies and Fairy Godmothers.

There is a problem with the latest Observation and the Actuator has materialised in two dimensional form and needs the help of his mechanic, currently in the form of a mouse, along with Maisie and her bigger, human hands, to complete the mission and prevent the complete extermination of the human race.

Maisie, along with her friends and parents, must in find a way for humanity to survive and free itself of its past
